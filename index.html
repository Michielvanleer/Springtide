My contribution

My co2 emission compensation scheme

co2 emission:300t

equivalent:100 trees

As I am very aware about my co2 emission coming from my lifestyle and from running my business I yearly compensate my emission by planting trees via Carbonfund. a share of my incoming from springtide.com is invested in renewed. Springtide can help you with your c02 calculations. Contact me contact or have a look at the "my contributions" page.

my contribution

My Environmental awareness training

One of the reasons for setting up this business if for me to fund and set-up an wide variety of practical and down to earth environmental awareness programs and especially one for childeren. They are the one who can be make a change, can adapt easier and have a great sense of wonder. I am a strong believer that a lot of damage to the planet can be avoided by people being aware about the impact of their actions. Maybe this is a reason for you to contact me contact or have a look at the "my contributions" page.

my contribution

Kids awareness training

Childeren can make a difference! In a playful and practical section we empower them to make a change.

Company awareness training

Either part of the requirements of certification or just to raise awareness. I will try to show the beauty of the oceans and the impact business processes have on the earth.

Public awareness training

We all have an impact, and we all can contribute to bring climate change to a stop. Small things do help. In this training, I make people aware about the change they can make in their daily actions.
