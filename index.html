About me

Hi, My name is Michiel van Leer and I am springtide. In 2017 I founded this business during my sabbatical, to be able share my experience and knowlegde with yourself, but also to generate income to kickstart the projects which you find in the my contribution section on this site. I worked for 8 years in the shipping, offhore and Oil and Gas sector as Environmental manager and found that, also seen the speed of Climate change I should extend my work field and contribute more extensively. Money is not my incentive, slowing down Climate change is. Read more about me
